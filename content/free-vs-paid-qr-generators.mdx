# Free vs Paid QR Code Generators: When Does Paid Make Sense?

Most people can get everything they need from a free QR code generator. Here's when free is enough—and when paid might be worth it.

## What Free Generators Offer

Free tools like [ours](/) typically provide:

- Encode any URL or text
- Download as PNG (sometimes SVG or other formats)
- No sign-up or account
- No usage limits for personal or small business use
- Static QR codes that never expire

That covers business cards, menus, flyers, packaging, events, and most everyday use cases.

## What Paid Generators Add

Paid services (often subscription-based) usually offer:

- **Dynamic QR codes:** Change the destination without reprinting
- **Analytics:** Scan counts, locations, devices
- **Bulk generation:** Create many codes at once via API or CSV
- **Custom designs:** Logo overlays, brand colors
- **Support:** Help desk, onboarding

## When Free Is Enough

Stick with free when:

- You need a few to a few dozen codes
- The content won't change (static is fine)
- You don't need analytics
- Simplicity and zero cost matter most

## When Paid Might Be Worth It

Consider paid when:

- You run marketing campaigns and need scan analytics
- You need to update destinations frequently without reprinting
- You generate hundreds or thousands of codes programmatically
- You want white-label or API access for your own product
